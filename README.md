#####Body#####

1. THE
LIFE
OF
Tomaso the Wanderer.

    _ PROEMIVM.

BEing to write Tomaso's Life, I know not
well what style to write it in; for, he's
too vicions for 
      * Of his Nature and Disposition.

      * How he Wandred Abroad.

      * How he was a Courtier.

      * How he was a Soldier.

      * Of his second Expedition.

      * Of his Writings.

      * Of his Religion.

      * L' Envoy.
